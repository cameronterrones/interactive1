About Red Hot Chili Peppers Smashing Pumpkins Alice in Chains Foo Fighters Frank Black Pearl Jam Nirvana Pixies

Ripped albums and low quality mp4s fed my preteen addiction to the loud and messy music of my fathers taste, thankfully through the development of technology I was able to expand and explore my fathers music via the internet. For my interactive time capsule project I created a catalog of miscellanies videos and albums I used to to explore the previous generations angst. However in order to download all of these albums and videos I was forced to leave much of the content behind. The tangibility of albums, the emotionally packed visuals of live performances, and the files of these artists were used to commemorate a time where convenience robbed us of an experience.
