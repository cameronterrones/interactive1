About Red Hot Chili Peppers Smashing Pumpkins Alice in Chains Foo Fighters Frank Black Pearl Jam Nirvana Pixies

Ripped albums and live performance videos from YouTube fed my teenage addiction to the loud and noisy music of my fathers generation. But before the low quality .mp4s was the CD. The tangibility of an album cover and the inserted booklets filled my time. These small experimental books were filled with rich abstract imagery that captured my attention. For my time capsule project I wanted to commemorate the loss of these tangible experiences. Moments that we trade for the convenience of digital media. By collaging these moments together to reflect nine of my favorite albums I hoped to capture a glimpse of these forgotten moments between physical and digital experience.
